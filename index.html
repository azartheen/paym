    Is there a cancellation?

    No. Our contract is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime.

    Do you offer refunds?

    Yes. You get a 100% Refund if you decide not to proceed after reviewing our first deliverable.

    Do you offer a refund for unused days of the month?

    No.Unused days are not refunded.

    Is it easy to cancel?

    Yes.Just send us an email and we will stop your billing cycle immediately.

    Is there a long-term contract?

    No. You can cancel anytime you like.

    Do you offer design services in Arabic Language?

    Yes we do. However we will require you to either purchase our translation services or provide us the content with instructions in English.

    Can I pay by Bank Transfer?

    Yes, we accept Bank transfers and cheques in addition to accepting all major credit cards online.

    Can I visit your office?

    Yes, but please make an appointment. We don't accept walk-ins due to the time-sensitive nature of our services.

    What types of designs are included?

    Depending on your package we cover a wide range of design products from social media banners, logos, ads, postcards, flyers to websites and app interfaces.

    How many design jobs can I submit in a month?

    Depending on your package, you can submit as many jobs as you want. All jobs are queued and will be delivered to you sequentially.

    Will you design and develop a full website from scratch?

    Absolutely. We have packages that include full website design and development in Wordpress environments.

    Do you custom backend coding?

    As far as the backend coding is within the Wordpress ecosystem we can handle backend coding.

    Do you handle Ecommerce websites, payment gateways integrations?

    Yes we do.

    How does it work?

    We will gather information about your brand / company, your campaign objectives and your ad budget. Our specialists will create a strategy document for your brand utilizing a mix of digital channels like search engine marketing, social media, etc. Once you approve the campaign plan, our specialist will implement as per agreed milestones.

    Will you write copy / content for my ads?

    Yes. All digital marketing plans including content and copywriting support.

    How many social media posts will you include in my plan?

    Depending on your campaign we will include a suggested number of posts in your custom plan.

    Is the ad spend included?

    No. You will be required to pay for your ad spend.

    When will you start working on my campaigns once I sign-up?

    We will start working on your campaigns once your approve the plan our specialists will build for your brand / company.

    How do you handle access and credentials to my Ad Accounts

    If you already have Ad accounts, our specialists will help you provide us access so we can analyze your marketing objectives.
